What is sauceDAO?

A community platform built for

A Decentralized Incubator!

We leverage our community to crowdsource good ideas. Our platform approaches the problem of incentivizing community funding and collaborative development to help pre-seed ventures reach their milestones.

We provide assistance in the form of specialized team building tools, open-source resources, funding opportunities, and access to a community of top talent.

A Project Accelerator!

Incubated projects get access to our community of talent and resources. While financial capital is vital for growth, human and social capital are often the backbone upon which successful ventures are built.

We collectivise and democratise these three key aspects into an accessible launchpad to bring ideas to life.

An Apprenticeship Program!

We pair new and motivated web3 developers with established software engineers to contribute to real world projects.

A Social Impact Fund!

We value people making real changes in their communities and prioritize supporting projects addressing quality of life issues.

Impact driven projects often lack the incentives to bring attention to the problems they address. Our approach harnesses the strength of the web3 community to empower social causes to bring about real change.
